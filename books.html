English Edition

THE PRIMACY OF SOFTNESS

A Non-Confrontational Philosophy of Being

Publication in preparation

Developed through long-term artistic practice, The Primacy of Softness proposes a non-confrontational philosophy of being grounded in resilience, relation, and continuity. Drawing from watercolor, lived experience, and sustained reflection, the book examines how strength may operate through Pause, Receive, Generate, Permeate, and Light.

Chinese Edition

柔性本元

一种非对抗的存在哲学

Publication in preparation

Rooted in long-term artistic practice and lived experience, 柔性本元 considers a structure of strength that does not depend on confrontation to prove itself. Beginning with the fluidity of watercolor, women’s concrete experience, and relations between people and the world, it explores relation, transformation, continuity, and how life maintains direction through Pause, Receive, Generate, Permeate, and Light.

中文版

柔性本元

一种非对抗的存在哲学

出版准备中

《柔性本元》源于长期的艺术实践与生命经验。作者从水彩的流动性、女性的具体经验以及人与世界的关系出发，提出一种不依赖对抗来证明自身的力量结构，并通过“停、承、生、渗、光”五个维度，讨论关系、转化、延续与生命如何在复杂现实中保持方向。
